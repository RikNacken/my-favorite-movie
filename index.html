-Django Unchained-

Django Unchained is Quentin Tarantino's seventh movie. Inspired by old spaghetti westerns. The movie won oscar for best originele scenario.


Synopsis

Two years before the Civil War, Django , a slave, finds himself accompanying an unorthodox German bounty hunter named Dr. King Schultz on a mission to capture the vicious Brittle brothers. Their mission successful, Schultz frees Django, and together they hunt the South's most-wanted criminals. Their travels take them to the infamous plantation of shady Calvin Candie, where Django's long-lost wife (Kerry Washington) is still a slave.

Cast/Crew

Written and Directed by Quentin Tarantino
